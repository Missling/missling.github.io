About me:

A Bay Area native that stumbled upon coding by sheer luck more than a year ago. Since then, I have fell in love and was fortunate enough to be accepted into Dev Bootcamp's 2015 cohort. Come join me on my adventure as I dive into the world of code, taking one loop at a time, all the while trying to avoid the dreaded infinite loop. My dream is to break barrier and transition from my current profession, finance, to the male-dominated industry that is tech.
